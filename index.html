Colombia is an amazing place with a plethora of activities to participate in and landmarks to visit. From its strong cultural community, to its beautiful cities and beaches, Colombia should definitely be on your next trip idea. Colombia has two different coasts both the Pacific and Caribbean with over 300 beaches to visit. Did you know, that Colombia is the 2nd most megadiverse country in the world, ranking first in its bird species! It has 10% of all the species of Fauna and Flora in the entire world! This country is one you won't forget, and one you hoped you could've stayed longer.
