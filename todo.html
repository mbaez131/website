What to do!

This Flower Festival takes place in Medellin, Colombia. To schedule a visit to this wonderful city during this annual time is the way to go. It has a pageant, horse parades, musical concerts, and float marches!

Go on an adventure hang gliding through the mountains, flying 1500ft above the water and paradise. This will definitely be experience you'll never forget.

Ready for unbelievable sight-seeing? Climb 650 stairs to reach the top of this beautiful rock formation, and enjoy by yourself or with your significant other a view to die for.

If you could think of the most beautiful beach cove in your mind, would it look something like this? This amazing beach location shows off the glittering caribbean water in contrast with the burnt orange sand. Stay until sunset to see the colors atop the smooth ocean water.
